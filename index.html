This is the story of my campaign journey.

Please click on the red dots on the map to read about the different stops on my campaign journey.

Between 2004 - 2012 I worked on nine different political campaigns. Lived in seven different states with a four-year pitstop to work on Capitol Hill in Washington, DC. Some of the races we won and some of them we lost. But all together it created an incredible journey with many amazing memories that I wanted to capture here on this page.

It started in Buffalo NY – my hometown – then off to New York City, Dubuque IA, Waterloo IA with pitstop in Washington DC. There I worked for Congressman Bruce Braley (IA-01) after we won his race in 2006. I worked for him for four years and then got the itch to go back out in the field and manage candidate races this time. I left DC in 2010 and took off to Manchester NH, Wilmington DE, Fairfax VA, Pittsburgh PA, and finally Columbus OH. I was always coming back to DC – after all that had always been the plan – make it to Washington, DC. I returned in 2012 from my incredible journey and still live and work in DC today.
